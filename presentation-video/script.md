# Amber & Oak Cafe Website Presentation Script

## Chapter 1 - Introduction
Hello, and welcome to the Amber & Oak Cafe website presentation. This is a professional restaurant landing website built for a premium cafe, restaurant, kopitiam, or small food business. The goal is simple: help customers understand the brand, browse the menu, view the atmosphere, find the location, and contact the business quickly.

The live website is published on Netlify at https://amber-oak-cafe-brian-bccm.netlify.app, and the source code is available on GitHub at https://github.com/Brian-bccm/AmberOakCafe.

## Chapter 2 - Homepage and Navigation
The homepage starts with a strong hero section. It shows the cafe name, a short value message, and two main actions: view the menu or order and reserve through WhatsApp. These actions are placed immediately because most restaurant visitors want to check food, price, location, and contact options quickly.

The navigation links move smoothly to each section: About, Menu, Gallery, Special Offer, Location, and Contact. On mobile, the menu changes into a compact button so the layout stays clean and easy to use.

## Chapter 3 - About, Menu, Gallery, and Promotion
The About section explains the cafe concept and creates trust. It highlights craft coffee, comfort plates, and a warm premium setting.

The Menu section shows realistic food items and prices, including Truffle Mushroom Toast, Smoked Duck Benedict, Nasi Lemak Croffle, Sea Salt Gula Melaka Latte, Charred Chicken Pesto Bowl, and Burnt Cheesecake. These can be changed later for a real client.

The Gallery section shows food, drinks, and cafe ambience. This is important for restaurants because customers decide quickly based on visuals.

The Special Offer section promotes a Weekend Brunch Set. This area can be reused for seasonal promos, festive menus, lunch deals, or opening offers.

## Chapter 4 - Location, WhatsApp, and Contact Form
The Location section gives the address, opening hours, parking and takeaway notes, and a Google Maps link.

The floating WhatsApp button stays visible while customers scroll. This helps turn visitors into reservations or orders without forcing them to search for contact details.

The contact form is connected through Netlify Forms. It validates required fields, shows a helpful message if something is missing, and confirms when the enquiry is ready.

## Chapter 5 - Mobile Experience
The website is designed to work well on phone and desktop. On mobile, the hero text becomes easier to read, buttons stack vertically, the stats stay compact, and the navigation becomes a mobile menu. This matters because many restaurant customers will open the site from WhatsApp, Instagram, Google Maps, or QR codes.

## Chapter 6 - Code Test and Deployment Proof
The project was tested with an automated test suite. The tests check the required sections, menu content, WhatsApp link, and contact form behavior.

The lint check confirms the code style is clean. The production build confirms the website can compile successfully for deployment.

The project is committed and pushed to GitHub, and Netlify reports the production deployment as ready.

## Chapter 7 - Client Handoff
For a real restaurant client, the content can be changed quickly. We can update the restaurant name, menu items, prices, images, address, opening hours, WhatsApp number, email, promotions, and brand colors.

This project is a strong freelance portfolio example because it solves real small business needs: professional branding, mobile-first layout, clear menu presentation, direct WhatsApp contact, contact form enquiries, and a live deployed website.
