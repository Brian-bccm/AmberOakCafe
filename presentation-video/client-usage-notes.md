# Client Usage Notes

## Live Links
- Website: https://amber-oak-cafe-brian-bccm.netlify.app
- GitHub: https://github.com/Brian-bccm/AmberOakCafe

## What The Website Does
- Presents the cafe brand in a premium first impression.
- Lets customers jump to menu, gallery, offer, location, and contact sections.
- Provides a floating WhatsApp button for fast orders or reservations.
- Includes a Netlify contact form for enquiries.
- Works on mobile and desktop.

## What A Client Can Change Later
- Restaurant name and tagline.
- Menu items, prices, and descriptions.
- Food and interior photos.
- Special offer or campaign.
- Address, opening hours, phone, email, WhatsApp number, and Google Maps link.
- Colors and style to match the real business brand.

## Suggested Client Testing
- Open the website on a phone.
- Tap every navigation item.
- Tap the WhatsApp button.
- Submit the contact form with a test message.
- Check if the menu prices and address are correct.
- Review all photos and promotional wording.
