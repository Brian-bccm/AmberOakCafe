# Client Usage Notes

This website is built for a restaurant, cafe, kopitiam, or small food business owner who wants a professional online presence without needing to understand code.

## How customers use the website

- Open the website from Google, Instagram, WhatsApp, QR code, or a shared link.
- View the homepage to understand the restaurant style.
- Tap Menu to check food, descriptions, and prices.
- View Gallery to see food and cafe atmosphere.
- Check Special Offer for current promotions.
- Check Location for address and opening hours.
- Tap WhatsApp for fast orders, reservations, or questions.
- Use the contact form for enquiries that need a written message.

## What the business owner can update later

- Restaurant name, logo, tagline, and story.
- Menu items, prices, descriptions, and best-seller labels.
- Food photos, cafe photos, and promotional images.
- Address, opening hours, Google Maps link, email, and WhatsApp number.
- Promotions such as lunch sets, festive menus, opening deals, or catering offers.

## Important handoff note

The client does not need to edit programming files. For normal updates, the client can send the new text, prices, photos, or contact details to the website provider, and the website can be updated for the business.
